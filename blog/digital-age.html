Digital Age

September 21, 2025

We live in the digital age while still having biologically caveman brains.

We need to navigate a world where we're always connected. That means a whole new way to socialize, where personal space is earned and worked for instead of being granted. The more you try to connect, the more isolated you can become.

There are whole new meanings and studies that need to be done for this mode of communication. We've solved the technology part, but biologically we can only adapt.

Is this just a long adjustment period until our brains catch up, or will we always live in that tension?
